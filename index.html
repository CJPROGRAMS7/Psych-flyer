Wellness Guide

Find Your Calm.

Stress is a natural part of life, but how we manage it makes all the difference. Discover healthy, sustainable ways to relieve tension and lower your cortisol.

Healthy Habits for stress Relief

Be Active

  • • Get Moving: Regular exercise like basketball or lifting weights burns off excess energy.
  • • Stay Focused: Focusing on a sport and activity can calm an anxious mind.
  • • Explore Outdoors: Fresh air and a quick walk or bike ride can reset your mood.

Read a Book

  • • Unplug: Swap screen time for a physical book to reduce eye strain and stress.
  • • Mental Break: Getting lost in a book provides a healthy escape from reality.
  • • Learn & Grow: Books provide an outlet for a busy mind and are very calming.

Get Social

  • • Connect: Spending quality time with friends helps you feel supported.
  • • Have Fun: Laughing and sharing positive moments is a natural mood booster.
  • • Join In: Participating in clubs or group activities builds a sense of community.

Avoid Unhealthy Habits

Late night doom scrolling, stimulants like energy drinks, or social isolation can increase anxiety. Choose to be active and productive instead.
